PROJECT NAME

Year: 2026 | Client: Editorial

The convergence of Bauhaus colors and stainless steel, we find a new resonance between humanity and machine. This project explores the boundary between the physical metal and the digital primary colors, creating a visual syntax that speaks to the future of design.

在包豪斯色彩与不锈钢的交汇点，我们找到了人与机器之间的新共鸣。本项目探索了物理金属与数字原色之间的界限，创造了一种对话未来设计的视觉语法。
